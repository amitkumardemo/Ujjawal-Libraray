Key Features

Explore the top features of Ujjawal Library that provide an ideal environment for your studies.

Bring Your Own Books

We don't provide books, but you're welcome to bring your own and dive into a world of knowledge. Our spacious reading areas are perfect for study sessions from 8 AM to 8 PM.

Comfortable Study Environment

Enjoy a comfortable study environment with air conditioning, fans, and a peaceful atmosphere. We also provide mineral water to keep you refreshed throughout your study hours.

Fast Wi-Fi & Charging Facilities

Stay connected and powered up! With fast Wi-Fi and charging boards available at every desk, you can work seamlessly without interruptions.

Games for Fresh Minds

Take a break with some fun games available to refresh your mind and come back to your studies with renewed energy.

200+ Seats Available

With over 200 comfortable seating arrangements, there is plenty of space for everyone to study, collaborate, or enjoy some quiet time.

Security & CCTV Monitoring

For your safety, we have CCTV cameras installed throughout the library, ensuring a secure and peaceful environment for everyone.
